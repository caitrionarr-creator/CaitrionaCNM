Strategic Development

Planning for future growth and technological advancement.

Strategic leadership & service development:

  • Service Establishment: Directed the complete establishment of an Electrophysiology (EP) Service, taking full responsibility for policy development, structural consultation, procurement, and staff training.
  • Capacity Growth: Delivered a 25% year-on-year service growth over two years through rigorous process optimisation, enhanced session utilisation, and strategic consultant scheduling.
  • External Collaboration: Built relationships with external hospitals and service providers to strengthen referral pathways and support hospital revenue generation. This includes GP relationships and external hospitals referring for transfer.
  • In-house CTCA Feasibility: Initiated a feasibility study for an in-house CTCA service following an audit of Rapid Access Cardiology Clinic data to address external referral leakage.
  • Multidisciplinary Leadership: Leading multidisciplinary consultations with Radiology and Business Management to evaluate software capabilities, cost structures, staffing, and clinical pathways.
  • Service Scoping: Partnering with a Consultant Cardiologist to scope service delivery and explore the integration of a private practice model.
